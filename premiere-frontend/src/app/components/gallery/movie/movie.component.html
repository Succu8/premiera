Morbius

horror, science fiction, action, thriller, fantasy

Premiere : 1 апреля, 2022

Duration : 108 минут

Director : Даниэль Эспиноса

Actor : Джаред Лето, Майкл Китон, Адриа Архона, Джаред Харрис, Мэтт Смит, Тайриз Гибсон

Michael Morbius has been suffering from a rare blood disease since childhood and has devoted his entire adult life to finding a cure. At some point, Michael sees a possible salvation in the blood of bats and decides on a risky experiment. The experience causes an unexpected reaction: by contacting the diseased cells, he changes the body of Morbius, turning him into a bloodthirsty monster.
